Overview

Purpose

Provide information about white water rafting to help create a safe and enjoyable experience for people who want to do white water rafting.

Audience

Who will access the website?
  People who are planning to do white water rafting or trying to learn about it.
Who are the target customers?
  People who have a desire to do white water rafting or want to learn more about it.
What age range should be required for white water rafting?
  Children should be at least 12 years old and be with a parent/guardian. Older people should only do white water rafting if they can do it safely.
What can prevent someone from doing white water rafting?
  Physical/mental disabilities might create unnecessary risks.
