Our goal it to provide a safe and enjoyable time for people who want to go rafting and meet the safety requirements.

Rafts, paddles, life jackets and other equipment are provided, to allow for people to participate in rafting without buying their own raft or equipment.

We provide a rafting instructor to teach a short lesson about rafting, before anyone gets on the river, to create a safe rafting trip for everyone in the group.
